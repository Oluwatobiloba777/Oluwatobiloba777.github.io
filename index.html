Dine In

Step into MamaBobo Restaurant and immerse yourself in a truly Nigerian dining experience. Our welcoming atmosphere is perfect for family meals, friendly gatherings, or a romantic dinner. Enjoy the rich tapestry of flavors that our authentic dishes offer, all served in a vibrant setting inspired by the beauty and culture of Nigeria. Whether it’s a casual lunch or a special dinner, dining with us is always an occasion to remember.

Delivery

Savor the flavors of Nigeria from the comfort of your own home with MamaBobo’s delivery service. We’ve partnered with Just Eat and UberEats to bring you your favourite dishes quickly and conveniently. Whether you're looking for a comforting meal at home or planning a feast with friends, our delivery service ensures that every order is packed with care and delivered right to your door. Explore our menu on Just Eat and UberEats and enjoy a taste of Nigeria anytime, anywhere.

Catering

At MamaBobo’s, we bring the unique flavors of Nigeria directly to your event with our bespoke catering services. Whether you are hosting a wedding, a corporate event, or a birthday, we offer customized menus that cater to a variety of tastes and dietary needs. Our dedicated team ensures that each dish is as delightful and memorable as your special occasion. Contact us to discuss how we can tailor our offerings to your event’s needs and make your gathering truly exceptional.

About Us

Welcome to MamaBobo Restaurant

"Embark on a culinary journey with us at MamaBobo Restaurant by sitting in or getting takeaway, where authentic Nigerian flavors come to life. As the first Nigerian restaurant in Northern Ireland, we're dedicated to bringing you the finest West African ingredients and delectable dishes. From our shelves stocked with vibrant spices and ingredients to our restaurant offering mouthwatering, healthy Nigerian cuisine, we invite you to savor the tastes of tradition and innovation. Experience the warmth of Nigerian hospitality and the vibrant tapestry of our culture through every bite."
